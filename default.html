Built-in Map Controls

Zooming: use the buttons on the map, the mouse wheel, or the +/- buttons on the keyboard. Hold the SHIFT key to drag a box. Pinch with 2 fingers on a touchscreen.

Panning: use the buttons on the map, or the arrow keys on the keyboard.

Double-click: double-click anywhere to zoom in one level.

Right-click: build your own map context menus with any functions you need.
